What is SciCrow 3000?

We are a Brazillian game design studio responsible for titles like Smith the Blue Rabbit and ISEKAI. Create games are our passion, for us games are not simply entertainment, they give life to what could not have such life, they make our imagination gain real colors, and our thoughts became voices that could be listened by everyone.

Our objective is to show to the world the Brazillian creativity by creating epic games that don't simply give some distraction to the players, but create a nostalgic feeling in their hearts as long they grow and live their lives. Our games are our true passion, and those experiences they bring to people must be memorable.

We don't simply create games, we create worlds!
